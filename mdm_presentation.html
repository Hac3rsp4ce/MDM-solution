Zero-Touch Deployment: Technical Magic Revealed

How Autopilot & ABM enable true hands-off deployment

ü™ü Windows Autopilot Process

1. Vendor Registration

OEM/Vendor uploads device Hardware Hash (unique ID) to Microsoft Partner Center and assigns to our organization

2. Autopilot Import

Hardware ID automatically appears in our Intune Autopilot devices list with our organizational assignment

3. Profile Assignment

We assign Autopilot deployment profile (apps, policies, configurations) to the device before shipping

4. User Experience

Employee powers on ‚Üí connects to internet ‚Üí device automatically contacts Autopilot service ‚Üí applies our configurations

üçé Apple Business Manager (ABM) Process

1. Vendor Enrollment

Apple Authorized Reseller automatically enrolls device serial numbers into our ABM account during purchase

2. MDM Assignment

ABM automatically assigns devices to our Intune/Hexnode MDM server based on pre-configured rules

3. Profile Preparation

We create enrollment profiles (apps, settings, restrictions) and assign to device groups in our MDM

4. Automatic Setup

Employee starts Setup Assistant ‚Üí device contacts ABM ‚Üí redirects to our MDM ‚Üí applies configurations automatically

üîß Vendor Integration Requirements

For Windows Devices:

  • Vendor must be Microsoft CSP partner
  • We provide our Microsoft tenant ID
  • Vendor uploads Hardware Hash to our tenant
  • No additional hardware configuration needed

For Mac Devices:

  • Vendor must be Apple Authorized Reseller
  • We provide our ABM customer number
  • Purchase must be made through authorized channel
  • Automatic enrollment on purchase

‚úÖ Result: True zero-touch deployment

Employee receives device ‚Üí powers on ‚Üí enters company email ‚Üí everything configures automatically without IT involvement
