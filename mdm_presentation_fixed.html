How Automatic Setup Works

The process behind the magic - explained in simple terms

ü™ü Windows Computers

Like pre-programming a universal remote

1. We Register with Vendor

Computer vendor adds our company information to each device before shipping (like putting our address label on a package)

2. Device Knows It's Ours

Computer automatically appears in our management system with our company settings ready to apply

3. We Set the Rules

We configure what apps, security settings, and policies should be applied to this device type

4. Employee Gets Magic Experience

Employee turns on computer ‚Üí connects to internet ‚Üí enters company email ‚Üí everything sets up automatically

üçé Mac Computers

Like having a personal shopping assistant

1. Vendor Auto-Enrollment

Apple-certified reseller automatically registers the device to our company account when we purchase it

2. System Assignment

Apple's system automatically connects the device to our management platform based on our pre-set rules

3. Profile Setup

We create setup profiles (like templates) with our company apps, settings, and security rules

4. Automatic Configuration

Employee starts Mac setup ‚Üí system recognizes it's ours ‚Üí automatically applies our company configuration

üîß What We Need from Vendors

For Windows Computers:

  • Vendor must be a Microsoft certified partner
  • We provide them our company ID number
  • They register each device to our account
  • No extra cost or setup time required

For Mac Computers:

  • Vendor must be Apple-certified reseller
  • We provide them our Apple business account
  • Purchase must go through Apple's system
  • Automatic enrollment happens at purchase

‚úÖ End Result

Employee receives device ‚Üí turns on ‚Üí enters work email ‚Üí everything configures automatically

No IT meetings, no manual setup, no delays!
